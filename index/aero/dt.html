_.5 | Inspection Start Point (ISP) |
_._ | ISP - начало инспекций, когда трещина становится видимой |
_._ | ISP=beta_det*/(Sd*Sc*Sa) |
_.__ | Sd=[-ln(1-z%)]^(1/alpha_d) | z=5-10% |
_.__ | Sc=[-ln(1-y%)]^(1/alpha_c) | y=1 out of group |
_.__ | Sa=[-ln(1-x%)]^(1/alpha_a) | x=1% |
_.___ | alpha(det/comp/airplane): 8/6/5(pressure); 6/5/4(ext.load) |
_.6 | beta_det |
_._ | beta_det=beta_com*(num_det)^(1/alpha_det) |
_._ | beta_com=N95*Scon*Srel*GAGdr |
_.__ | DFR (from fatigue) -> N95 |
_.__ | Scon=1.3 - 95% confidence factor | Srel=2.1 - 95% reliability factor |
